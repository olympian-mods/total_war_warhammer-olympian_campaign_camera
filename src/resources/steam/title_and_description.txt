TITLE
-----

Olympian Campaign Camera


DESCRIPTION
-----------

A campaign camera mod for Total War: Warhammer with adjusted height parameters.
It allows the camera to zoom further out on the campaign map and a little bit closer down to ground level.

Supports "Standard" and "Classic" campaign camera settings.

For more extreme camera parameters try the [url=http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=691803600]WAAAGH Edition[/url] of this mod.

If you're looking for a battle camera mod I recommend [url=http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=690476829]Better Battle Camera[/url] by kam2150 or [url=http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=690896921]RSG Battle Camera[/url] by RoYaL sPaRtAn GuArD.
